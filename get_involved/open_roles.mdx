As an early-stage startup, we have limited corporate jobs available.  
Nevertheless, we're always hiring exceptional talent.

## How to get noticed by our engineering team

* The easiest way to get our attention is to build great chatbots and datasets.
* The second easiest is to be an active, supportive community member in our discord.
